Nazm-e-Adil (The Archives of the Self)

Nazm-e-Adil is a digital archive and personal anthology documenting a 135-day poetic journey through love, existential crisis, and self-discovery.

Overview

This project presents a chronological collection of Urdu poetry (with English translations) divided into two distinct volumes, tracing the transformation from an architect of illusion to a refugee of reality.

Volume I: The Descent (Sept 3 - Oct 13, 2025)

Theme: The construction of a romantic and spiritual ideal. The frantic search for the Beloved in every aspect of existence ("Har Naam Mein Tu") and the eventual exhaustion of that search.

Key Works: Har Naam Mein Tu, Khud Se, Woh Hoti Toh Kya Hota.

Volume II: The Void (Oct 16 - Jan 15, 2026)

Theme: The deconstruction of the self. The acceptance of exile ("Muhaajir"), the realization of the "flaw" as a feature, and the final descent into silence and "Pragmatic Mysticism."

Key Works: Lahad, Gard-e-Jahaan, Shab-e-Sha'oor.
